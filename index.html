Meet Our Character

Our story follows a rural student who faces challenges accessing digital education resources. Through this character, we explore the barriers to inclusive digital education and how our architecture helps overcome them.

The Learning Journey

Follow our character's path from limited connectivity to full engagement with digital education resources. This journey illustrates how each layer of our architecture supports inclusive access.

  • Discovery: Finding educational resources despite connectivity challenges
  • Access: Obtaining content through multi-modal delivery systems
  • Engagement: Participating fully in the learning community
  • Mastery: Demonstrating knowledge acquisition through accessible assessment

Narrative Frameworks

These storytelling elements create meaningful connections between learners and educational content:

  • Personal journey stories highlighting diverse experiences
  • Problem-solving narratives engaging critical thinking
  • Interactive storylines adapting to different learning preferences
  • Community-based narratives connecting learning to real-world contexts
